03 — The mechanism

How the funnelworks

Every customer scans one QR code. Happy customers are guided straight to Google. Unhappy ones are quietly caught before they post — so you fix the problem privately instead of publicly. On Growth & Pro, we also send automated WhatsApp review requests a few hours after each visit, with a gentle follow-up if there's no reply.

Scan the code
